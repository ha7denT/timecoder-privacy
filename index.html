Timecoder Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 9, 2026

Overview

Timecoder is a timecode calculator and video logging tool for macOS. Your privacy is important to us.

Data Collection

Timecoder does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data.

The app operates entirely on your device. No information about you or your usage is sent to any server.

Local Data

Timecoder stores your preferences (such as default frame rate and marker color) locally on your Mac using standard macOS preferences. This data never leaves your device.

Video Files

Video files you open in Timecoder are processed locally on your device. They are never uploaded or shared.

Analytics

Timecoder does not include any analytics or tracking software. The only analytics available are Apple's standard App Store metrics, which are anonymized and aggregated by Apple.

Third-Party Services

Timecoder does not integrate with any third-party services.

Changes
